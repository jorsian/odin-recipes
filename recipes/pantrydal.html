Pantry Dal

Description

This pantry dal is a version of indian dal that is loaded with veggies. This is a great option if you have leftover veggies in your fridge that need to be used up. The coconut milk and turmeric makes this a rich and filling recipe. It's very adapatable and most of the ingredients can be modified to suit your tastes. This dish is vegan and gluten free and is recommended to be served with brown rice and cilantro as a garnish

Ingredients

  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 6 cups choice of veggies, chopped
  • 3 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 cup water
  • 3 cups bean of choice, cooked

Directions

  1. Begin by sauteing the onion for 4 minutes with oil or water.
  2. Slowly add the chopped veggies, adding in first the vegetables that take longest to cook. Leafy greens should not be added at this point. Saute for five minutes or until vegetables are tender
  3. Toss vegetables with curry powder and salt for two minutes
  4. Add coconut milk, diced tomatoes and water
  5. Bring to a boil
  6. Let simmer uncovered for 10-15 minutes, until vegetables are cooked down
  7. Add beans and leafy greens if desired
  8. Mix and serve with brown rice

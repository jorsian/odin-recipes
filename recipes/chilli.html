Teal's Black Bean Chilli

Description

This Black Bean Chilli is a recipe by spiritual catalyst Teal Swan. This dish is hardy and rich, vegan and gluten free. The levels of spice can be adjusted according to taste. Presoaking the beans is optional, and any type of beans can be used as an alternative to black beans. Ideal for a cold winter day and best served with avocado, cilantro and vegan cheddar as a garnish.

Ingredients

  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 tbsp. cumin seeds
  • 1 carrot, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 tbsp. salt
  • 2 tbsp. cumin powder
  • 1 tsp. oregano
  • 2 cups frozen corn kernels
  • 6 cups black beans, cooked
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp. lemon juice (fresh)
  • opt. brown rice

Steps

  1. Saute the onion and cumin seed with oil or water until the onion is translucent and the seeds are dry and fragrant
  2. Add the bell pepper, carrot, salt and cumin powder and cover for 10 minutes or until everything is soft
  3. Add the oregano, frozen corn kernals, black beans, water and crushed tomatoes
  4. Uncover and let simmer for 35 minutes
  5. Serve with brown rice, avocado, cilantro and vegan cheddar if desired

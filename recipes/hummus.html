Hummus

Description

This hummus is a quick version of the classic recipe and can prepared in as little as five minutes. In my house this is a staple, and I have a glass dish of it in my fridge all the time. Hummus is versatile and can be served with fresh vegetables as a dip or as garnish with a main course. This particular hummus is oil free, vegan and gluten free

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup tahini
  • juice of two lemons
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp. cumin
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 cups chickpeas, cooked

Steps

  1. In a food processor add tahini and lemon juice.
  2. Process for one minute. Scrape down sides.
  3. Process for another minute.
  4. Add salt, cumin and water.
  5. Process for one minute. Scrape down sides.
  6. Add a third of the chickpeas and process for a minute. Scrape down sides
  7. Add another third of the chickpeas and process for a minute. Scrape down sides.
  8. Add last third of the chickpeas and process for one more minute. Scrape down sides.
  9. If the hummus is too thick, add more water and process for another minute.
  10. Serve with fresh vegetables of your choice, or as a topping on your favourite meal.
